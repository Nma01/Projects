Ezigbo Nne m

Just like you travel and say to me "Nma, don't worry. I am returning in a few days time".


Few days have turned into few weeks, few weeks into few months, few months into few years.
Mum, many few years have gone by, still no sign of you.


I have been patiently waiting for you to come back. It's now 7years mum, you still have not returned like you promised.



When are you coming home mummy?
